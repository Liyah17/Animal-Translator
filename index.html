About Us

Our app, called (ai) animal intelligence, is designed to help you better understand your pet's needs and emotions. By connecting your device to our special collar, you can translate your pet's sounds and behaviors into understandable language.

About Our App

Why Does This App Cost $10?

Our app is priced at $10 due to the investment in development and maintenance. We strive to deliver a high-quality user experience, which requires significant resources and expertise.

Important Information

When using our app, please ensure that you provide accurate information, especially regarding your pet's breed. Providing incorrect information may affect the app's ability to process your pet.

Instructions

To use our animal translator collar, simply attach it to your pet's collar and activate the app on your device. Follow the on-screen instructions to calibrate the collar to your pet's unique vocalizations. Once calibrated, you'll be able to understand your pet's needs and feelings.
